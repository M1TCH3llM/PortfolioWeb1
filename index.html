    • About

    I'm Mitchell Morgan, a passionate UX designer and full stack web developer with a hunger for design challenges. My journey began with a UX design certification from Google and a full stack boot camp at Denver University. While I'm new to the industry, my background in graphic design enriches my creative perspective.

    I approach design with the user at the center, valuing efficiency and simplicity. Proficient in Figma, Adobe XD, Photoshop, and Illustrator, I bring aesthetics and functionality together. On the development side, I use HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to create seamless web experiences. Beyond work, I find inspiration in outdoor hikes, workouts, art, and quality time with my dog, Maya.

    My goal is to create impactful digital experiences, prioritizing user-centric design and innovation in every project I undertake.
